Who am I?

I am a passionate developer with expertise in creating beautiful and functional web applications. As a UI/UX designer and web designer, I specialize in crafting user-centered digital experiences that are visually compelling, intuitive, and responsive.

Beyond design and development, I am also skilled in data analysis and machine learning using Python. I enjoy turning raw data into actionable insights and building intelligent systems that solve real-world problems.

With a strong blend of creativity, technical knowledge, and analytical thinking, I am committed to delivering solutions that not only look great but also perform exceptionally.
